.. _woa:

World Ocean Atlas v2
====================

Description
-----------
The World Ocean Atlas (WOA) is a data product of the Ocean Climate
Laboratory of the National Oceanographic Data Center. It consists of
objectively analyzed climatological mean fields at 1 degree spatial
resolution. The climatology is based on all data that are available from
the early 1900s to present.

Source
------
- `World Ocean Atlas v2 Website`_

Release Policy
--------------
Cite the World Ocean Atlas 2013 for the data sets used (see references).
The World Ocean Atlas 2013 (and all previous versions) are available free
of charge.
